Organic spacer cations bearing &minus;NH<sub>2</sub> groups preferentially bind to under-coordinated oxygens on metal oxide substrates, inducing spontaneous 2D perovskite crystallization at the buried interface that reduces surface recombination velocity by 72% and enables up to 22.9% PCE in inverted solar cells.
