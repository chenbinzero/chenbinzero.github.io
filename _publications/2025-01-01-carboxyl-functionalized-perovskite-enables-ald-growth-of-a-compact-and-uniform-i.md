5-AVAI carboxyl groups on the perovskite surface act as ALD nucleation templates, enabling compact Al<sub>2</sub>O<sub>3</sub> growth at 100 &deg;C and a 10&times; reduction in halide migration into the C<sub>60</sub> ETL, with 1.78 eV cells retaining 90% PCE after 1000 h of continuous operation at 55 &deg;C.
