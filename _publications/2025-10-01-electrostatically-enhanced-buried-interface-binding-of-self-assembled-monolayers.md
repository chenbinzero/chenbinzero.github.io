The donor-acceptor SAM PAFTB, with its enhanced dipole moment and tailored anchoring groups, strengthens electrostatic interactions at the TCO/perovskite buried interface 2.8&times; relative to 2PACz, yielding certified 24.9% quasi-steady-state PCE with 80% efficiency retention after 900 h of MPP tracking at 85 &deg;C.
