NiP porphyrin supramolecules self-assemble at perovskite grain boundaries, where organic cations convert them into hole-transporting ammonium porphyrins that simultaneously passivate defects and promote inter-grain hole transport, yielding certified 22.1% PCE on 1.0 cm<sup>2</sup> with stability exceeding 3000 h under illumination or at 85 &deg;C.
