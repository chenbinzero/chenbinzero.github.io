Amidinium ligands exploit resonance-enhanced N&ndash;H bonds to resist deprotonation under light and thermal stress, reducing the deprotonation equilibrium constant &gt;10-fold and enabling certified 26.3% PCE with &ge;90% PCE retention after 1100 h of continuous 1-sun MPP operation at 85 &deg;C.
