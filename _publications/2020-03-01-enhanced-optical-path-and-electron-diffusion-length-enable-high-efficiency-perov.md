A boosted solvent extraction method increases perovskite film thickness while preserving smooth morphology, and a Lewis base additive extends the electron diffusion length to 2.3 &mu;m, enabling 19% semi-transparent top cells and 28.2% perovskite/silicon tandem solar cells.
