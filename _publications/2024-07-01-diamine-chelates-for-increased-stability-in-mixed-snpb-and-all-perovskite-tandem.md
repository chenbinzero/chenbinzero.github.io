Diamines selectively chelate surface Sn atoms in mixed Sn&ndash;Pb perovskite films to correct a Sn-rich compositional gradient, forming a low-dimensional passivating barrier that enables 28.8% PCE tandem solar cells retaining 90% efficiency after 1000 h of MPP operation under 1-sun illumination.
