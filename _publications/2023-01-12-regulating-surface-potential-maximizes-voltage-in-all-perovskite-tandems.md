Diammonium surface treatments achieve uniform spatial distribution of surface potential in 1.79 eV perovskite subcells, increasing quasi-Fermi-level splitting by 90 meV and enabling a certified 27% all-perovskite tandem with a record V<sub>OC</sub> of 2.19 V and &gt;86% PCE retention after 500 h.
