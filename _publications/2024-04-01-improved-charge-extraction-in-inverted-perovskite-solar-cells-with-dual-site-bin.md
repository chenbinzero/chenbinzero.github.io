Ligands designed to bind two neighboring Pb<sup>2+</sup> defect sites in a planar orientation reduce steric resistance at the perovskite surface, achieving a certified 26.15% quasi-steady-state PCE (24.74% for 1.04 cm<sup>2</sup>) with 95% PCE retention after 1200 h of continuous 1-sun MPP operation at 65 &deg;C.
