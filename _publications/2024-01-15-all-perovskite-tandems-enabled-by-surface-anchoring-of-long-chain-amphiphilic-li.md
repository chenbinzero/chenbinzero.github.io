Oleic acid treatment of Pb&ndash;Sn perovskite surfaces coordinates Sn<sup>2+</sup> sites and reduces interfacial recombination, lowering the V<sub>OC</sub> deficit to 0.34 V and enabling certified 26.4% all-perovskite tandem solar cells with a V<sub>OC</sub> of 2.21 V.
