Ab initio molecular dynamics simulations uncover a reversible hydrogen-vacancy degradation mechanism at perovskite surfaces and reveal that dynamic adsorption affinity&mdash;rather than static binding energy&mdash;is the key predictor of passivation efficacy, guiding design of targeted ligands for enhanced photovoltaic stability.
